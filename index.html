Methodology

Life Expectancy

We use 77 years, the average life expectancy in the United States according to CDC data (2022). This varies by country, gender, and lifestyle factors.

Source: CDC National Center for Health Statistics

Waking Hours

We assume 16 waking hours per day, based on the average adult sleeping 7-9 hours. Social media time is calculated as a percentage of waking hours, not total hours.

Source: Sleep Foundation

Books Read

Average book length is ~90,000 words. Average reading speed is 238 words per minute. This means ~6.3 hours per book. We round to 6 hours to account for focused reading.

Source: Brysbaert, M. (2019). How many words do we read per minute?

Language Learning

The US Foreign Service Institute estimates 600-750 hours for Category I languages (Spanish, French, Italian) to reach "Professional Working Proficiency." We use 600 hours for conversational fluency, which is a lower bar.

Source: U.S. Department of State Foreign Language Training

Limitations

This visualization assumes consistent daily usage over remaining lifetime. It doesn't account for: productive social media use, usage changes with age, or time that might be spent on other passive activities instead. The goal is awareness, not precision.
